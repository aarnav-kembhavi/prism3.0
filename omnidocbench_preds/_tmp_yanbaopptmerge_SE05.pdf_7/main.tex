\subsection*{Human Factors}

\begin{itemize}
\item the process molds to the needs of the people and
team, not the other way around.

\item key traits must exist among the people on an agile
team and the team itself:.

\item Competence.

\item Common focus.

\item Collaboration.

\item Decision-making ability.

\item -Fuzzy problem-solving ability..

\item Mutual trust and respect..

\item Self-organization.

\end{itemize}
